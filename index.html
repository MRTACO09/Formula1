Description

Formula 1 Racing is a fun two person game where you try to go around the track as many times as possible before your opponent.

How To Play

If you are drivng the red car than you use WASD. And to change gears you use Q as the clutch and gears are 123.

If you are driving the blue/black car than you use Arrow Keys. And to chnage gears you use P as the clutch and gears are 890.

Training Mode

This is where you can test your skills and try to get your best lap time possible. This is a one person mode if you cannot find someone to play with.

Race Mode

This is where you can play against another person in the room. Once you have completed the first lap the lap counter will appear. You and your partner can make a collective descion on how mnay laps to do. (This is not the finished product, so no cheating! 😏)
